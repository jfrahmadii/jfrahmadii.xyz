Ontario's Grid: Clean, but Drifting

Transmission-connected generation mix & carbon trajectory · 2020–2025 · Source: IESO
The story in five seconds: Ontario runs one of North America's cleanest grids — but its emissions-free share has slipped from 93.2% (2020) to 80.1% (2025). Natural-gas generation has tripled (10.1 → 32.2 TWh) and nuclear output dipped during the refurbishment window, while renewables held flat near a third of supply.
